=====================
Contact Us
=====================

The `Contact Us page <https://www.illinoislegalaid.org/contact-us>`_ allows visitors to reach ILAO staff with questions, feedback, and general inquiries.

ILAO is an online nonprofit that provides clear, easy-to-understand legal information and forms for civil legal issues in Illinois. ILAO does not provide direct legal representation.

Before contacting ILAO
=======================

The page directs visitors with specific needs to other resources before submitting the form:

* **Legal help** — visitors seeking a lawyer or legal services should use `Get Legal Help <https://www.illinoislegalaid.org/get-legal-help>`_
* **Volunteering** — prospective volunteers should visit the Our Team page
* **Media inquiries** — press contacts should email the External Relations Director directly via the link on the page

Contact form
=============

The contact form is a Drupal webform with the following fields:

* Name
* Email
* Subject
* Message
* Zip code
* Category (dropdown, required)

Contact categories
-------------------

The dropdown includes:

* I want to leave a compliment
* I need help with my account
* I want to suggest a form or other content be created
* I found a problem with the information on this website
* I found a problem using this website
* I am a partner organization and am seeking materials, training, or other support

Submission routing
===================

Submissions are routed by category to the appropriate ILAO staff member or team inbox.
